


Bryan Cranston’s Memoir Drops in October

By Dianna Dilworth on May. 5, 2016 - 4:06 PMComment

Breaking Bad star Bryan Cranston is writing a memoir.

Titled, A Life in Parts, the book will chronicle the story of Cranston’s life from early childhood through becoming a cult icon.

In the book from Simon & Schuster’s Scribner, Cranston will tell the story of his acting career from soap operas to Seinfeld and Malcolm in the Middle, and from playing President Lyndon Johnson on the stage to playing a chemistry teacher turned drug kingpin on Breaking Bad.

The book hits bookshelves October 18th.
