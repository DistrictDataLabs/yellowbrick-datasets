





Ricardo Lockette is walking away from football.

NFL Media Insider Ian Rapoport reported on Wednesday that the Seattle Seahawks wide receiver is retiring. 

The wideout posted a message on social media about his decision.

Lockette sustained ligament damage in his neck in Seattle's Week 8 win against the Dallas Cowboys last season.

The Seahawks have scheduled a news conference with Lockette on Thursday.
